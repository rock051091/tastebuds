Spicy Honey Citrus Chicken

Spicy Honey Citrus Chicken is the perfect healthy grilling recipe! Made with simple ingredients and full of flavor, this easy chicken marinade is delicious! Gluten free and paleo - this chicken dish is a must try!

Course Main Course
Cuisine American
Keyward citrus chicken, honey citrus chicken
Prep Time 5 minutes
Cook Time 15 minutes
Marinating Time 30 minutes
Total Time 50 minutes
Servings 4 people
Calories 298kcal
Points ★ ★ ★ ★ ★
Author Taste Buds

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil

  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest

  • 1 tablespoon orange zest

  • 1 tablespoon lime zest

  • 2 tablespoons orange juice

  • 2 tablespoons lime juice

  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice

  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes

  • 2 tablespoons honey

  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

  • 1.5 lbs chicken breasts

Instructions

  1. Whisk together marinade.

  2. Pour marinade into a ziploc bag or container.

  3. Add chicken to marinade and allow it to marinade for at least 30 minutes. (Overnight is best!)

  4. When ready to cook, heat grill on high and cook on each side for 7-9 minutes or until chicken is cooked thoroughly (internal temp should be 165)
